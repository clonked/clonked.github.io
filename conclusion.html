I want to thank you for reading this log on the Holy Land cruise. It by no means has been easy making it. A good four months this project was under development, undergoing many changes, some the eyes can see and other 'behind the scenes' deals. Over 150 pages of HTML, 500 separate images and countless man hours have been put into this. Its my first true masterpiece. I have to say I hope you have enjoyed reading and exploring this guide as much as I did making it (though there are times I'd have rather just played games). And you know something? Doing something like this makes you realize exactly how much you appreciate a trip like the Holy Land cruise, it's truely a once-in-a-lifetime deal.
