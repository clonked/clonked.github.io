The ten people that set off for the Holy Land cruise all have names and faces. This page acts as a small introduction to each of the ten members of the Family Federation (as named way back when). Enjoy learning about my fellow mates. You should note that these have been arranged in no particular order of importance.

Tim is a pharmaceutical man by trade. He's a quiet, caring and generous man that has a loving wife and three kids. He enjoys reading, fishing and to be in nature.

Wife to Tim; Heidi is an affectionate, self-assured woman. A mild-mannered housewife by day, at night she turns into a vivacious cooking machine that's sure to please. She enjoys nature, reading and playing games.

He's picky, pessimistic and the creator of this webpage. That means nothing but good is uttered about this handsome gentleman, not as if that wasn't already the case. Nathan enjoys relaxing, being lazy and eating.

Funny, shy, not outgoing; Drew is the epitome of how to be an attention recluse. You can bet he'll be the serious one in the group. He enjoys running flight simulations, playing sports and hoarding food products in natural disasters.

Tyler is crazy, off-the-wall and downright looney. The few times he is normal, however, he makes for an ok guy. He spends his time sitting in pitch black rooms mumbling to himself and biting his arm.

Former waitress gotten sucked into the fast-pace business world, Kerree is on top of things. She enjoys large books of hidden word puzzles, collecting rocks and having a good laugh.

Mother to all the women on the trip except Jan, Rogene is a proud, loving lady. She loves animals, in particular cats. Rogene enjoys reading, learning historical information and social gatherings.

John is a crazy, funny and all around weird guy. He collects disco albums and claims to have authored a few of his own songs. John is also an interior designer. He enjoys knocking on wood, organizing sock drawers and traveling.

Wife to John, Shelley is a fun, take action kind of girl. She is an interior designer along with her husband. In her spare time Shelley likes to watch movies, plan and go on trips and spend time with
her family.

John's mother; Jan is an exciting get up and go woman. She lives in California and occasionally has been spotted in Omaha, Nebraska. Her interests are as of now unknown.

The crazy old man that only Tyler can see, Willy will follow Tyler around til his deathbed. Willy goes with Tyler on his adventures and gives him invaluble advice on life and love.


That concludes the travel roster for the Mediterranean vacation. Though one or two old-school members were missing from the ranks, the group certainly had the potential to make the trip an awesome one. And that they did. You can now turn the page to start reading about the Holy Land trip, which suitingly began with our weekend in Istanbul; or if you aren't sure on something, hop back to the Introduction.
