elcome to Mediterranean Cruise Adventure. This page documents the events of the Holy Land cruise our family went on from Saturday, November 27th to Saturday, December 11th, 1999. We have arranged the information on this page in a way we feel to be as simple as possible. You will read this document the way you read a book, by turning pages. This is done by clicking the small blue arrows found in the lower corner's of each sheet of paper. The images that are integrated into these documents will open in a new window, if you want to see a blown up view, so as not to interupt your reading. Note that the account you will read is as unbiasedly written as possible, in order for you to see what actually happened. To discover people's specific opinions, follow the link indicated at the end of documents applying to this constraint.

To go between pages, use the left and right arrows at the bottom of every page. If there is no left or right arrow that means you cannot go backward or forward, respectively. Next to the arrow will be a small piece of text indicating the main theme of the upcoming page. You should note that in this document, and all HTML documents for that matter, things only need to be single clicked. Double clicking is totally unnecessary.

From all of us here at Clonked! Productions, we hope you enjoy this online guide to the Holy Land cruise as much as we did making it for you!
